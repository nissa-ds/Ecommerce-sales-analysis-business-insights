# Ecommerce-sales-analysis-business-insights
Exploratory data analysis of e-commerce sales data to uncover revenue trends, customer behavior and actionable business insights.

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales data to identify key revenue drivers, customer purchasing patterns, and time-based sales trends. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that can support data-driven decision-making.

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Understand customer purchase behavior
- Detect seasonal and monthly sales trends
- Generate insights useful for business growth and strategy

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains transactional e-commerce sales records, including:
- Order details (Order ID, Date)
- Product and category information
- Sales, profit, and quantity metrics
- Customer-level purchasing data

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas – data manipulation
- NumPy – numerical analysis
- Matplotlib & Seaborn – data visualization
- Jupyter Notebook – analysis and presentation

## Key Analysis Performed
- Sales and profit trend analysis
- Category-wise and product-wise performance
- Monthly and yearly revenue patterns
- Customer contribution to total sales
- Identification of high-value products and segments

## Business Insights
- A small set of products contributes disproportionately to total revenue
- Certain product categories consistently outperform others
- Sales show clear seasonality across months
- Customer purchase frequency impacts overall profitability
- These insights can help businesses optimize inventory, marketing strategies, and pricing decisions.
